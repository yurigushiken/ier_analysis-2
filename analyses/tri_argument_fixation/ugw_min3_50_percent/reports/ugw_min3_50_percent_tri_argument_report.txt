Research Question: Do participants in each cohort fixate all relevant arguments during Upside-down give with toy events (50% on-screen trials)?
Hypothesis: Infants younger than 10 months will fail to fixate all three arguments more often than older infants and adults.
Prediction: 10+ month infants and adults will show higher tri-argument fixation rates than 7–9 month infants.

Results:
- 7-month-olds: 1/7 trials (14.3% coverage, 2 participants)
- 8-month-olds: 4/9 trials (44.4% coverage, 4 participants)
- 9-month-olds: 6/13 trials (46.2% coverage, 4 participants)
- 10-month-olds: 13/20 trials (65.0% coverage, 7 participants)
- 11-month-olds: 9/18 trials (50.0% coverage, 6 participants)
- Adults: 21/46 trials (45.7% coverage, 14 participants)
