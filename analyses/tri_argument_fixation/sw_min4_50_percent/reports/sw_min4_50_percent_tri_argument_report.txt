Research Question: During SHOW-with-toy events (min4 threshold, 50% on-screen trials), do cohorts fixate all three arguments?
Hypothesis: Older infants and adults will reach tri-argument success faster than younger infants.
Prediction: 10+ month infants and adults will outperform 7–9 month infants.

Results:
- 7-month-olds: 9/22 trials (40.9% coverage, 7 participants)
- 8-month-olds: 11/23 trials (47.8% coverage, 7 participants)
- 9-month-olds: 7/20 trials (35.0% coverage, 6 participants)
- 10-month-olds: 15/33 trials (45.5% coverage, 11 participants)
- 11-month-olds: 16/26 trials (61.5% coverage, 8 participants)
- Adults: 28/46 trials (60.9% coverage, 14 participants)
