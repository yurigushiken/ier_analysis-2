Research Question: Do participants in each cohort fixate all relevant arguments during GIVE-with-toy events (50% on-screen trials)?
Hypothesis: Infants younger than 10 months will fail to fixate all three arguments more often than older infants and adults.
Prediction: 10+ month infants and adults will show higher tri-argument fixation rates than 7–9 month infants.

Results:
- 7-month-olds: 7/11 trials (63.6% coverage, 4 participants)
- 8-month-olds: 8/15 trials (53.3% coverage, 5 participants)
- 9-month-olds: 14/25 trials (56.0% coverage, 8 participants)
- 10-month-olds: 24/37 trials (64.9% coverage, 11 participants)
- 11-month-olds: 11/13 trials (84.6% coverage, 5 participants)
- Adults: 28/39 trials (71.8% coverage, 13 participants)
